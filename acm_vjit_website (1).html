Who We Are

About ACM VJIT

The ACM VJIT Student Chapter is a vibrant community of computing enthusiasts at Vignana Jyothi Institute of Technology, Hyderabad. We are the official student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery — the world's largest scientific and educational computing society.

Our Story

Building Tomorrow's Tech Leaders

Founded in 2018, ACM VJIT began as a small group of passionate students who wanted to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world technology. Today, we are a thriving community of 340+ members spanning all branches of engineering.

We host workshops, hackathons, seminars, and mentorship programs that equip students with the skills needed to excel in the technology industry. Our members have gone on to work at leading tech companies including Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and innovative startups.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Goals

🎯 Mission

To create a dynamic learning environment that empowers students with technical skills, research exposure, and professional development to excel in the global computing community.

🔭 Vision

To become the most impactful student computing chapter in South India — a hub where innovation meets education and students transform ideas into world-class solutions.

🚀 Goals

Host 50+ technical events annually, develop 100+ industry-ready graduates, establish research collaborations, and mentor the next wave of computing entrepreneurs.
